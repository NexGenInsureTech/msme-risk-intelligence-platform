# MSME Risk Intelligence Platform — User Journey

## Purpose

This document defines the end-to-end experience of users interacting with the MSME Risk Intelligence Platform.

The journey is designed to guide MSMEs through a structured discovery process that identifies business risks and recommends appropriate insurance protection.

---

# Primary Users

1. MSME Business Owner
2. Insurance Underwriter
3. Insurance Broker / Advisor

---

# Platform Entry Experience

Landing Page Message:

"Discover the risks hidden in your business and protect your balance sheet."

Primary Action Button:

Check Your Business Risk Score

---

# MSME User Journey

## Step 1 — Business Introduction

User provides basic information:

• Business name  
• Industry type  
• Location  
• Years of operation

Purpose:

Identify industry risk baseline.

---

## Step 2 — Risk Discovery Advisor

User answers guided questions.

Topics covered:

• assets and machinery  
• inventory and facilities  
• operational dependencies  
• employee exposure  
• customer interactions

Purpose:

Identify operational and liability risks.

---

## Step 3 — Balance Sheet Mapping

User provides financial indicators:

• major assets  
• supplier dependencies  
• revenue concentration  
• recovery time from disruptions

Purpose:

Evaluate financial risk and business resilience.

---

## Step 4 — Risk Engine Analysis

The platform processes the inputs using:

• risk taxonomy  
• balance sheet framework  
• risk scoring model

Outputs generated:

• operational risk score  
• financial risk score  
• liability risk score  
• continuity risk score

---

## Step 5 — Risk Intelligence Report

The platform generates a structured risk report.

Includes:

• overall MSME risk score  
• top risk exposures  
• balance sheet vulnerabilities  
• risk heat map

---

## Step 6 — Insurance Recommendation Engine

Based on risk exposures the system recommends coverage:

Examples:

• Fire & Special Perils  
• Machinery Breakdown  
• Public Liability  
• Business Interruption

---

# Insurer / Broker Journey

Insurance professionals access the platform dashboard.

Capabilities:

• view MSME risk profiles  
• analyze underwriting intelligence  
• review recommended coverage  
• generate insurance proposals

---

# Platform Value

For MSMEs:

• risk awareness  
• business resilience insights  
• insurance guidance

For insurers:

• structured underwriting intelligence  
• improved risk assessment  
• better pricing decisions
