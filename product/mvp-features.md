# MVP Feature Definition

## Purpose

This document defines the minimum viable product (MVP) for the MSME Risk Intelligence Platform.

The MVP is designed to validate the platform concept quickly with insurance brokers and MSME businesses.

The objective is to build the smallest working version that delivers meaningful risk intelligence.

---

# Target MVP Users

Primary User

Insurance Brokers

Secondary User

MSME Business Owners

---

# Core MVP Capabilities

The MVP includes five primary capabilities.

---

## 1 MSME Profile Creation

Brokers can create a new MSME profile.

Inputs:

- business name
- industry type
- location
- years of operation

Purpose:

Establish the business context for risk analysis.

---

## 2 Risk Discovery Advisor

Guided discovery questionnaire.

Covers:

- assets and machinery
- operational risks
- employee exposure
- customer interactions

Purpose:

Identify operational and liability risks.

---

## 3 Balance Sheet Risk Mapping

Collect financial indicators.

Examples:

- asset concentration
- supplier dependency
- recovery time from disruption

Purpose:

Identify financial risk exposure.

---

## 4 Risk Intelligence Engine

The system calculates:

- operational risk score
- financial risk score
- liability risk score
- business continuity risk score

Output:

Overall MSME risk score.

---

## 5 Risk Intelligence Dashboard

Display risk insights directly on the screen.

Includes:

- risk score
- risk category breakdown
- identified exposures
- recommended insurance coverage

---

# Features Excluded From MVP

The following features will be introduced in later versions.

- downloadable risk reports (PDF)
- insurer integrations
- automated underwriting
- AI risk prediction
- API integrations

---

# MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is considered successful if:

- brokers can assess MSME risks quickly
- risk insights help guide insurance recommendations
- insurers find the intelligence useful for underwriting
