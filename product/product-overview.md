# Product Overview

The MSME Risk Intelligence Platform is composed of two main systems.

1. MSME Risk Discovery Interface
2. Insurance Intelligence Dashboard

## MSME Discovery Journey

Step 1: Business identity  
Step 2: Asset identification  
Step 3: Risk exposure analysis  
Step 4: Risk score generation  
Step 5: Insurance recommendation

## Output

- MSME risk score
- risk exposure map
- recommended insurance coverage
