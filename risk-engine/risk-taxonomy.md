# MSME Risk Taxonomy

## Purpose

This document defines the structured classification of risks faced by MSMEs.  
The taxonomy forms the foundation of the Risk Intelligence Engine used to:

- analyze MSME exposure
- calculate risk scores
- recommend appropriate insurance coverage
- support underwriting decisions

---

# Risk Categories

The platform classifies MSME risks into six primary domains.

1. Property Risk
2. Operational Risk
3. Machinery & Equipment Risk
4. Liability Risk
5. Employee Risk
6. Business Continuity Risk

---

# 1 Property Risk

Risks affecting physical assets.

Examples:

- Fire
- Explosion
- Flood
- Earthquake
- Storm damage
- Theft
- Vandalism

Relevant Insurance:

- Fire & Special Perils
- Property Insurance

---

# 2 Operational Risk

Risks affecting day-to-day operations.

Examples:

- Power failure
- Supply chain disruption
- Utility outages
- Vendor dependency
- Technology failure

Relevant Insurance:

- Business Interruption
- Contingent Business Interruption

---

# 3 Machinery & Equipment Risk

Risks affecting production equipment.

Examples:

- Machinery breakdown
- Electrical faults
- Boiler explosion
- Equipment overheating
- Maintenance failure

Relevant Insurance:

- Machinery Breakdown Insurance
- Boiler & Pressure Plant Insurance

---

# 4 Liability Risk

Risks where the business may be legally responsible.

Examples:

- Product defects
- Customer injury
- Third-party property damage
- Professional errors

Relevant Insurance:

- Public Liability Insurance
- Product Liability Insurance
- Professional Liability Insurance

---

# 5 Employee Risk

Risks involving employees or workers.

Examples:

- Workplace injury
- Occupational illness
- Accidents during operations

Relevant Insurance:

- Workmen Compensation
- Group Personal Accident

---

# 6 Business Continuity Risk

Risks that can stop the business from operating.

Examples:

- Natural disasters
- Major fire damage
- Key supplier failure
- Regulatory shutdown

Relevant Insurance:

- Business Interruption Insurance

---

# Taxonomy Usage in the Platform

The risk taxonomy supports three platform functions.

1. MSME Risk Discovery Journey
2. Risk Score Calculation
3. Insurance Recommendation Engine

Each discovered risk exposure contributes to the overall risk score.
