# MSME Risk Discovery Questions

## Purpose

This document defines the guided discovery journey used by the platform to understand the risk exposure of an MSME.

The discovery journey is designed as a **friendly advisor experience**, helping business owners identify risks before recommending insurance solutions.

---

# Discovery Journey Structure

The journey consists of five stages.

1. Business Identity
2. Asset Understanding
3. Operations Risk
4. People & Liability Risk
5. Business Continuity Risk

---

# Stage 1 — Business Identity

Question 1  
What type of business do you operate?

Options:

- Manufacturing
- Retail / Shop
- Service business
- Warehouse / Storage
- Food processing
- Other

Question 2  
How long has your business been operating?

Options:

- Less than 1 year
- 1 – 3 years
- 3 – 10 years
- More than 10 years

Question 3  
Where is your business located?

Inputs:

- City
- State
- Industrial / Commercial / Residential area

---

# Stage 2 — Asset Understanding

Question 4  
What valuable assets does your business rely on?

Options:

- Machinery
- Inventory / stock
- Electronics / equipment
- Building / premises
- Vehicles

Question 5  
If these assets were damaged, how long would it take to recover?

Options:

- Less than 1 week
- 1–4 weeks
- 1–3 months
- More than 3 months

---

# Stage 3 — Operational Risk

Question 6  
What could interrupt your business operations?

Options:

- Fire
- Power failure
- Machinery breakdown
- Supplier delays
- IT system failures

Question 7  
Do you rely heavily on a single supplier or vendor?

Options:

- Yes
- No
- Multiple suppliers

---

# Stage 4 — People & Liability Risk

Question 8  
How many employees work in your business?

Options:

- 1–5
- 5–20
- 20–50
- 50+

Question 9  
Do customers visit your premises?

Options:

- Frequently
- Occasionally
- Rarely

Question 10  
Do you manufacture or sell products that customers use?

Options:

- Yes
- No

---

# Stage 5 — Business Continuity Risk

Question 11  
If your business had to stop operations suddenly, what would be the biggest impact?

Options:

- Revenue loss
- Employee wages
- Customer commitments
- Loan repayments

Question 12  
Have you experienced any major disruptions before?

Options:

- Fire incident
- Flood / natural disaster
- Machinery failure
- None

---

# Platform Output

Based on responses, the platform generates:

- MSME Risk Score
- Risk Exposure Map
- Insurance Recommendations

Example output:

Risk Score: 72 / 100

Top Risks Identified:

- Fire hazard
- Machinery breakdown
- Employee injury exposure

Recommended Insurance:

- Fire & Special Perils
- Machinery Breakdown
- Workmen Compensation
