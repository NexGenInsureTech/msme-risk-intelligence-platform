# Balance Sheet Risk Framework

## Purpose

This framework connects an MSME's financial structure with its operational risk exposures.

The balance sheet is treated as a **risk barometer**, where disruptions affecting assets, liabilities, or revenue streams directly impact business stability.

The framework supports two goals:

1. MSME education and risk awareness
2. Data-driven risk intelligence for insurers

---

# Core Principle

Business resilience can be evaluated through the relationship:

Assets = Liabilities + Equity

Risk events impact businesses in three primary ways:

• Asset damage  
• Liability expansion  
• Revenue interruption

The platform analyzes these exposures and recommends insurance solutions.

---

# Balance Sheet Risk Domains

The system evaluates five financial risk domains.

1. Asset Exposure
2. Revenue Dependency
3. Liability Exposure
4. Operational Dependency
5. Financial Stability

---

# 1 Asset Exposure

Risks affecting business assets.

Examples:

• Fire damage to facilities  
• Machinery breakdown  
• Inventory loss  
• Theft

Relevant Insurance:

• Fire & Special Perils  
• Machinery Breakdown  
• Stock Insurance

---

# 2 Revenue Dependency

Risks affecting income generation.

Examples:

• supplier disruptions  
• IT system failure  
• business shutdown due to disasters

Relevant Insurance:

• Business Interruption  
• Contingent Business Interruption

---

# 3 Liability Exposure

Risks where the business may face claims.

Examples:

• product defects  
• customer injury  
• professional errors

Relevant Insurance:

• Public Liability  
• Product Liability  
• Professional Liability

---

# 4 Operational Dependency

Risks arising from key business dependencies.

Examples:

• reliance on key supplier  
• dependence on key employee  
• logistics interruptions

Relevant Insurance:

• Key Person Insurance  
• Supply Chain Insurance

---

# 5 Financial Stability

Risks related to financial resilience.

Examples:

• high debt exposure  
• limited cash reserves  
• revenue concentration

Relevant Insurance Support:

• Trade Credit Insurance  
• Business Continuity Planning

---

# Platform Application

The balance sheet framework integrates into the discovery journey.

Step 1  
Collect operational data.

Step 2  
Map exposures to balance sheet categories.

Step 3  
Calculate financial risk score.

Step 4  
Generate insurance recommendations.

---
