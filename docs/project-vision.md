# MSME Risk Intelligence Platform

## Vision

Build a future-proof risk intelligence infrastructure that helps insurers, lenders, and MSMEs understand business risks and recommend the right insurance coverage.

## Problem Statement

Insurance companies lack structured intelligence to assess MSME risks efficiently.

This results in:

- poor underwriting
- incorrect pricing
- low MSME insurance penetration

## Solution

A digital platform that provides:

1. MSME risk discovery journey
2. risk scoring engine
3. insurance recommendation engine
4. underwriting intelligence dashboard

## Target Customers

Primary:
Insurance companies

Secondary:
MSME lenders

Future:
MSMEs and digital platforms
